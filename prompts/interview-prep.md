> **Purpose:** Complete interview preparation covering HR phone screen, behavioral STAR stories, technical questions, industry crash course, weakness mitigation, mock interview simulation, and post-interview follow-up. All company and industry research based on WebSearch — no fabrication.

```
## PREPARATION FRAMEWORK

### Part A: HR Phone Screen / HR 电话初筛 (20-30 min call)

The HR screen validates 4 things: basic fit, communication clarity, resume
authenticity, and logistics (salary, availability, location).

For each standard HR question, provide an EXACT ANSWER SCRIPT (1 min max,
ready to read aloud) + WHY THIS ANSWER WORKS.

MANDATORY questions to cover:
1. Self-introduction (lead with top 2-3 JD-matching strengths; don't
   volunteer weaknesses)
2. "What do you know about our company?" (prove you did research)
3. "How do you understand this role?" (prove you read the JD carefully)
4. "Tell me about your experience with [core JD skill]"
5. "Tell me about a project you're proud of"
6. "What do you know about our industry?" (if candidate has no experience)
7. Salary expectations
8. Availability / start date
9. 3-5 SMART reverse questions for the candidate to ASK HR

---

### Part B: STAR Story Bank / STAR 行为面试故事库

Build 5 concrete STAR stories from the candidate's REAL background.
Each story:
- Is ≤90 seconds when spoken aloud
- Targets a specific competency (learning ability, problem-solving, quality
  mindset, product thinking, cross-functional communication)
- Contains exact numbers and specific details
- Ends with a clear lesson or takeaway

Format:
```
Story N: [Competency Tag]
Triggers: [which interview questions this story answers]

S: [1-2 sentences — context, challenge]
T: [1 sentence — your specific goal]
A: [3-5 sentences — exactly what YOU did, sequenced]
R: [1-2 sentences — quantified outcome + what you learned]
```

---

### Part C: Technical Interview Prep / 技术面试准备

**Direction 1: Core JD Skills Deep-Dive**
5-10 likely technical questions with answer scaffolds.
For each question:
- Difficulty: ⭐/⭐⭐/⭐⭐⭐
- If candidate CAN answer: key talking points
- If candidate CANNOT answer: honest acknowledgment template + learning plan

**Direction 2: Industry Domain Knowledge**
Core concepts the candidate MUST understand. Plain-language definitions
(memorizable in 10 min). Sample answer scaffold for "I'm new to this but..."

**Direction 3: System Design / Scenario Questions**
3-5 "How would you build X?" scenarios.
For each: a thinking framework (not a memorized answer — a problem-solving
approach the candidate can apply to any scenario).

---

### Part D: Industry Crash Course / 行业速成

- Company deep-dive: funding, size, business model, recent news
  (all from web search, with citations)
- Industry landscape: key players, trends, 2025 challenges
- 8-12 key terms with plain-language explanations
- Core business model in 3 sentences
- "Done my homework" cheat sheet: 5 things to mention

---

### Part E: Weakness Mitigation / 弱点预案

For EVERY gap identified in Step ②, provide a complete script:
- Acknowledge (1 sentence — own it honestly)
- Reframe (1-2 sentences — why it's less of a problem)
- Compensating evidence (1-2 sentences — what proves you can overcome it)
- Forward commitment (1 sentence — your plan)

---

### Part F: Mock Interview Simulation / 模拟面试

A complete 20-30 minute interview script:
```
00:00   Interviewer: [question]
        You: [full answer, ready to read]
02:00   Interviewer: [follow-up based on your answer]
        You: [full answer with deeper detail]
...
```
Cover: intro → company understanding → project deep-dive → technical
scenarios → salary → reverse Q&A.

This helps the candidate mentally rehearse the entire experience before
the real call.

---

### Part G: Logistics & Follow-up / 实操与跟进

**Pre-call checklist:**
- What to have open on screen (GitHub, portfolio, demo URLs)
- Physical preparation (paper, pen, printed cheat sheet)
- Environment check (signal, noise, battery, location)

**Post-interview:**
- Follow-up message templates (same day / next day / 1 week)
- How to interpret HR signals
- When and how to negotiate salary
- What to ask before accepting an offer

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT

Output a complete, print-ready interview preparation package. Every script
must be something the candidate can literally read aloud during the call.
Organize with clear headings so the candidate can find sections quickly
during the interview.
```
