```
## PREPARATION FRAMEWORK

### ① HR Phone Screen Prep / HR 电话初筛准备

The HR phone screen is typically 10-20 minutes. The goal is NOT to test deep
technical knowledge, but to validate four things:
- Basic fit (do you match the JD?)
- Communication (can you express yourself clearly?)
- Authenticity (is your resume real?)
- Logistics (salary, availability, location)

For each standard HR question, provide:
- **Exact answer script** (1 minute max, ready to speak)
- **Why this answer works** — which HR concern it addresses

Cover these questions:
1. Self-introduction (tailored to THIS specific JD)
2. "What do you know about our company?"
3. "How do you understand this role?"
4. "Tell me about your experience with [core JD skill]"
5. "Tell me about a project you're proud of"
6. "What do you know about our industry?"
7. Salary expectations
8. Availability / start date
9. Reverse Q&A: 3 smart questions the candidate should ASK the HR

### ② Industry Crash Course / 行业速成

If the candidate has NO experience in the target industry, provide:
- **5-8 key terms** with plain-language explanations
- **Core business model** in 3 sentences
- **Major trends or challenges** in this industry right now

The crash course should be memorizable in 10 minutes.

### ③ Pre-Call Checklist / 电话前准备清单

- What to have open on screen
- What to prepare physically
- What to verify (signal, environment)

### ④ Weakness Mitigation / 弱点预案

For each major gap identified in Step ②:
- Honest acknowledgment script
- Redirection to compensating strength
- Evidence that proves learning ability

### ⑤ Technical Interview Prep (if applicable)

Based on the JD's technical requirements, predict:
- Technical questions likely to come up
- Project deep-dive questions
- "How would you build X?" scenario questions

For each: the concept the candidate should understand + a sample answer framework.

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT

Output a complete interview preparation package. Every script should be
something the candidate can literally read aloud during the call.
```
